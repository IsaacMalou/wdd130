Basic HTML Structure

What Are Semantic Tags?

Semantic tags describe the meaning and purpose of the content they contain.

When you read the tag name, you can understand what kind of information is inside.

These tags make your HTML code easier

to read and help screen readers assist people with visual impairments.

What Are Non-Semantic Tags?

Non-semantic tags do not provide information about the meaning of their content.

They are primarily used for styling and layout purposes.

The two most common non-semantic tags are div and span.
